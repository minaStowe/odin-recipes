Tatooine Bug Juice Cocktail Recipe

Step right up, intrepid travelers of the cosmos, and behold the wonder that is the Tatooine Bug Juice Cocktail! Crafted from the exotic essences of Tatooine's finest insects, this libation is a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you buzzing with excitement. Picture yourself lounging under the twin suns, sipping on a concoction that captures the essence of the desert planet's vibrant ecosystem. With a dash of sand beetle zest, a pinch of krayt dragon spice, and a splash of dewback droplets, each sip is a journey through the untamed wilderness of Tatooine. So whether you're a daring podracer or a seasoned moisture farmer, raise your glass to the Tatooine Bug Juice Cocktail : a true testament to the wild spirit of the galaxy's most notorious desert world!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz melon liqueur
  • 2 oz sour mix
  • 1 1/2 oz lemon or lime juice
  • Lemon-lime soda
  • 2 maraschino cherries with stems
  • Soda water (optional)

Instructions

  1. Shake melon liqueur, sour mix, and lemon or lime juice in a shaker.
  2. Pour into glass and top with lemon-lime soda. If too sour, dilute with soda water.
  3. Garnish with maraschino cherries, making sure stems are pointing up like an insect antennae.
