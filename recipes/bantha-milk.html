Bantha Milk Recipe

Blue Bantha milk is the intergalactic equivalent of a cool, refreshing splash on a scorching Tatooine day! Squeezed straight from the udders of those majestic Banthas, it boasts a hue as vibrant as a swirling nebula. Picture it: a group of Jawas kicking back after a hard day's scavenging, clinking their glasses filled to the brim with that electric blue elixir. Whether you're a moisture farmer, a droid on the run, or a Jedi in training, nothing beats the feeling of quenching your thirst with a tall glass of Bantha bliss!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 tbsp blue fruit punch powder
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • blue food coloring (as needed)

Instructions

  1. Combine milk, ice, fruit punch powder, and vanilla in a blender. Cover and blend until smooth.
  2. Stir in a few drops of blue food coloring until desired color is achieved.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
