Spotchka Cocktail Recipe

Ah, Spotchka! It's the fizzy, frothy delight that lights up every Wookiee's eyes and tickles every Twi'lek's taste buds! This legendary brew, brewed from the mysterious depths of the forest moon of Endor, is a bubbly concoction that dances on your tongue like a playful Ewok in the trees. Imagine a mug filled with the essence of Endorian flora, infused with the zest of adventure and the thrill of the unknown. Whether you're celebrating a successful podracing victory or simply unwinding after a long day dodging blaster fire, Spotchka is the beverage of choice for those who dare to explore the far reaches of the galaxy!

Ingredients

  • 4 oz yellow-colored energy/sports drink with vitamin B12
  • 1 oz vodka
  • 1 oz blue curacao
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • Ice cubes
  • Black light (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix vodka, blue curacao, energy/sports drink, lime juice, and ice cubes.
  2. Optional: Shine black light on drink to add a glowing effect.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
